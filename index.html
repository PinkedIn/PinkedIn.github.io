FAQ

What is this?

This is a service for subscribing to alerts for job postings that match your search criteria, from companies that match your political alignment.

Where is this for?

Any country, but city-level resolution for political alignment is US-only (for now).

How is this working?

We try to identify the company and look up its political donations; if we can't identify the company, we use the city/state/country of the flagship and the political donations of that locale.

Why is this?

A recent survey says the following: 1) Over half of workplaces put agenda ahead of profit; 2) The biggest predictor of workplace cohesion/productivity/morale is a strong sense of common purpose. You should be able to feel good about who your work is enriching, and feel good about being part of the group (for the 50% of your life you spend working).

Who is this?

A Fortune 50 ESG corporation demanded I declare my religion and to quote the religious ruling that precludes me from taking experimental gene therapy rather than HCQ/IVM, in order to be exempt. They fired me the next day. (And no, I don't have the money to fight corporate lawyers.) While looking for my current job, I wrote this website to help me in my job search. I hope I can help you too.

When is this from?

Ha ha. I know how to write "modern" bloated single-page applications in Angular/React/jQuery. It doesn't matter if a site is "reactive" if it requires an initial massive CPU/RAM/network spike for dozens of API calls which don't execute in parallel, themselves handed off to microservices that hand off to other microservices. (Not to mention these obnoxious heartbeat timers and metrics tracking requests executing once per second). Do these bootcamp graduates bandwagoning on the latest JS framework not know what "abandonment rate" is? This entire site comes in at 60KB of parallel GET requests for cached static content; a mobile GPU renders it instantaneously. Moreover: Unemployed people donate the little they have with the understanding that I'm going to aggressively minimize hosting costs. I take that responsibility very seriously.

What do donations go towards?

I accept donations only to defray hosting costs. I run this at a loss. If I get in excess, I plan to use it to add a feature to surface hidden unadvertised job opportunities.
